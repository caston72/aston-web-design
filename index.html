A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in San Antonio, TX, with my family of four. My journey into tech started with a degree in education. As I started working as a Career & Technical Education teacher, my intrest grew for technology and the web. I was fascinated with coding and loved solving problems as I was learning. I was hooked and wanted to build a career from my passion for code.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my problem solving skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code to bring the website in your imagination to life.
